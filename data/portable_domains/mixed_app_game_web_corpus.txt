# Atlas Notes mobile app manual
Atlas Notes keeps a local encrypted cache of the five most recent notebooks so writing stays available offline. When the device reconnects, sync resumes automatically and conflicts are shown in the Sync Review panel.
The quick capture shortcut is Ctrl+Shift+N on desktop and a two-finger swipe down on mobile. Quick captures are saved to the Inbox notebook and can be tagged later.
To export a notebook, open Share, choose Export, then select Markdown or PDF. Exports include tags, backlinks, and embedded image captions.
If search feels stale, open Settings, Search, and tap Rebuild Index. Rebuilding the local index does not delete notebooks and usually completes in under a minute.

# Northwind Shop website support notes
Northwind Shop accepts returns within thirty days when the item is unused and the order receipt is available. Open the order page and choose Start Return to print the prepaid label.
Guest checkout orders can be tracked with the order number and email address on the Track Order page. Account holders can also see carrier scans in Order History.
Subscription deliveries can be paused from Account, Subscriptions, Pause Delivery. Pausing keeps the discount active and resumes on the selected date.
For damaged packages, upload two photos and the shipping label in the support form. The support team can reship the item or issue a refund after review.

# Ember Road combat guide
Before entering a dangerous room, stabilize health, choose the first target, keep a fallback lane open, and avoid spending every cooldown immediately.
When a combat mistake happens, stop chasing damage, retreat or guard to stabilize, remove the closest threat, then rebuild tempo with one safe card.
If two enemies arrive at once, create space first, identify the faster threat, disable or kite it, and rotate after the lane is safe.
When Pip is alone after the player is downed, Pip should survive first, avoid melee traps, clear the active wave safely, and retreat until a clean attack opens.
