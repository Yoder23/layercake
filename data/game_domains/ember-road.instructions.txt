Question: Give a concise plan before entering the next room. Answer: Enter with health stable, pick the highest-threat enemy first, keep one escape lane open, and save a defensive card for the first bad trade.
Question: How should I recover after a mistake in combat? Answer: Stop chasing damage, retreat or guard to stabilize, remove the nearest threat, then rebuild tempo with one safe card.
Question: Explain the safest first step when two threats appear. Answer: Create space first, identify the faster threat, disable or kite it, then rotate to the second threat after the lane is safe.
Question: What should Pip do when the player is downed? Answer: Pip should survive first, avoid melee traps, clear the active wave safely, and use flanking or retreat until a clean attack opens.
Question: How should I handle an archer behind a brute? Answer: Flank or advance across lanes to pressure the archer, avoid the brute's melee range, and do not let the archer fire freely.
Question: What is the safe response to a boss telegraph? Answer: Guard or move out of the marked lane, wait for the committed attack, then punish the recovery window with burst damage.
Question: When should I use mend? Answer: Use mend below half health when no immediate kill is available, but do not waste it at high health or when defense is more urgent.
Question: When should I use guardLink? Answer: Use guardLink when heavy damage is coming, a brute is close, or the player is exposed and needs time to reposition.
Question: How do I fight a shaman? Answer: Treat the shaman as a priority target, interrupt summons, move off purple ground markers, and pressure from a different lane.
Question: How do I fight the Ancient Treant? Answer: Read ground cracks, preserve health between phases, clear summoned creatures, and burst during recovery or Heartwood Expose.
Question: What is the best general combat rule? Answer: Preserve position first, remove priority threats second, and only spend burst when it changes the state of the fight.
Question: How should I enter a dangerous room? Answer: Stabilize resources, choose a first target, keep a fallback lane open, and avoid spending every cooldown at the start.
Question: What should I do when surrounded? Answer: Break the surround by moving vertically, guard if a hit is unavoidable, then focus the weakest enemy to reopen space.
Question: How should I use cards efficiently? Answer: Use cards to reset tempo, interrupt threats, or secure kills; do not spend cards just to stay busy.
Question: How do I protect both heroes? Answer: Keep crossfire, avoid stacking in one lane, guard during enemy burst, and heal only when it prevents a losing trade.
