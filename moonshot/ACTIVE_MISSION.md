## Permanent lock

The promoted LayerCake core must use raw UTF-8 bytes or a fully deterministic,
tokenizer-free byte-patching contract. BPE, SentencePiece, WordPiece, unigram
tokenization, and any learned external segmentation are prohibited.

After prompt prefill, every continuation byte must be chosen from the frozen
model's neural output distribution by a declared decoding policy. Planners,
templates, forced next tokens, predetermined continuations, retrieval, stored
answers, copied corpus chunks, and deterministic response transducers are
prohibited.

One checkpoint must simultaneously establish quality, CPU speed, memory,
incremental execution, physical sparsity, and generation behavior. Phase 2
requires at least a 2x optimized-transformer CPU throughput advantage, median
latency no greater than 0.60x, no worse time to first useful output, lower active
memory, and sustained advantage through 1,024 generated bytes.

Phase 3 may begin only after the corrected Phase 2 release is committed, sealed
under `layercake-moonshot-phase2-r2`, and independently verified. Phase 3 must
preserve the exact Phase 2 architecture and prove at least 2x lower wall-clock
time to the same frozen general-quality threshold while retaining at least 95%
of certified Phase 2 CPU throughput.

## Required reread points

Before architecture selection, a new training campaign, a benchmark change,
candidate promotion, certificate generation, or final response, reread:

1. `AGENTS.md`
2. `moonshot/ACTIVE_MISSION.md`
3. `moonshot/campaign.yaml`
4. `results/moonshot/phase2_recertification/task_state.json`

No historical favorable number overrides these contracts.
